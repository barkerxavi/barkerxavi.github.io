Custom Vector Fields in Houdini

To start its best to find some reference as its difficult to predict how a volume might move.

Once you have a good video or at least an image, figure out how the volume is moving and what is causing it (is there a fire causing an updraft or high windshear for example)

For me I found a video of a large oil fire that was twisting and skewed off to one side due to the wind.

Below you can see how I created velocity volumes to mimic the twisting motion.

At first i took inspiration from an impeller and used it to create a similar shape around the center of where I wanted my fire.

Then I converted it to points using the tangent as a velocity vector.

I moved these points in a random direction just a bit and then used a point replicate to fill out the area a bit.
